Ryan Kellogg

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Stanford University jointly advised by Profs. Michael Snyder and Stephen Quake (Genetics and Bioengineering departments, respectively). My work unites engineering and biology to understand how cells process information and make decisions. During my PhD I studied noise and digital activation in the NF-κB system using microfluidics and mathematical modeling. I currently work on how the microbiome communicates with the immune system.
